Our Story

As the leading cinema exhibitor in Malaysia with over 509 screens across 55 locations nationwide, Golden Screen Cinemas (‘GSC’) delivers innovative, integrated, and enriching lifestyle experiences through state-of-the-art cinematic technologies , merchandising, F&B, events, and more.

We have

  • PlayHalls, specially designed for families with kids;
  • BIG Screens, complete with big format screens and spatial, crisp audio;
  • IMAX with Laser Halls, the first of its kind in Malaysia, bringing action-packed movies to life, with vibrant colour contrast and sharper visual clarity;
  • Happy Food Co; a lively space for you to satisfy your cravings while catching up with your loved ones, and perfect for some ‘me’ time.
  • Keepsake; a one-stop e-commerce shop for movie-lovers to purchase their favourite movie merchandise;
